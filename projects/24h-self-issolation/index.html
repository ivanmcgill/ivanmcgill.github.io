24h: Self issolation

First edition 2020, second edition 2024. Printed and published by Último Mono (Seville).

Photos: Andrea Carilla

2 risograph inks, 24 pages, A5 size. Edition of 100.

Comic improvised during the 24 hours lockdown organized by Sputnikat press during the COVID pandemic.  A person voluntarily locked in a virtual reality that allows him to satisfy everything he is not able to supply on the physical plane.
