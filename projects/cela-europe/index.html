CELA-Europe

2024. CELA is a talent development program that offers a European context to a new generation of literary creators across 11 different countries. Art Directed by Erik Peters.

A map composed of portals to give a place to the literary texts that inhabit the reading platform. This series of illustrations function as different realms that help to classify the material in this literary archive.
