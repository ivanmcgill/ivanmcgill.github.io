Les jardins des données

Solo exhibition at the Musée Urbain Tony Garnier in Lyon. France.

The Tony Garnier Urban Museum, committed to supporting young creators, presents Les jardins des données: my first exhibition in France.

The Tony Garnier Urban Museum, created thirty years ago through the initiative of the residents of the Cité Tony Garnier to protect and enhance their architectural heritage, has since explored diverse themes related to urban life and housing. Dedicated to promoting culture and art in its many forms, the museum has partnered this time with Lyon BD and FICOMIC to design an exhibition addressing the theme of the city in comics.

The exhibition explores the transformation of society and our living spaces in the era of new technologies, driven by big data and artificial intelligence. I created a world of data where we follow the misadventures of three characters: Aya, Jason, and Gharb. As we move through the museum's rooms, we discover their world, how it functions, and delve into their hyperconnected lives.

My deepest gratitude to the Tony Garnier Urban Museum, Lyon BD, and FICOMIC, who have made this exhibition possible.
