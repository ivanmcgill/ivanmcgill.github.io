Therapy

First edition 2022, second edition 2023. Publised by Fatbottom comics (Barcelona).
Printed by Máquina Total, Barcelona.

Photos: Andrea Carilla

1 risograph ink, 24 pages, A4 size Edition of 100

A conversation between a patient lost in himself and his controversial therapist. A journey through self-knowledge and the way to find the tools to keep going.
